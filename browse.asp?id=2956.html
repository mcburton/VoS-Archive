UCSB Resources
English Literature  
California Digital Library Provides online access to the databases of the shared collections of the University of California libraries
Chadwyck-Healy Individual Literature Collections
Early English Books Online (institutional subscription required; contains scanned images of over titles listed in Pollard & Redgrave's Short-Title Catalogue, 1475-1640, Wing's Short-Title Catalogue, 1641-1700, as well as the Thomason Tracts, 1640-1661)
Early English Books Online Text Creation Partnership (EEBO-TCP) Fully searchable database of 2,800 EEBO texts.
Information Resources for English A guide to UCSB's electronic indexes, abstracts and reference sources
MLA International Bibliography Indexes of articles journals, series, proceedings, reference works and dissertations from 1963 to the present (via California Digital Library)
Oxford English Dictionary Online Access to the online version of the OED
Oxford Text Archive Allows access to well-documented, high quality digital text resources in a number of languages
University English, and The Literary Encyclopedia (lists English-department home pages, job vacancies, publishers, and the Literary Encyclopedia; plans to include a journals database, and lists of scholarly associations and conferences) (Robert Clark, UEA Norwich)
University of Virginia Electronic Text Center Provides texts and databases for literature in many languages
