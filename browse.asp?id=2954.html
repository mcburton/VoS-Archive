Individual Film Sites  
Elizabeth Images from the film, information on filming, script and cast, plus links on the film and its subject matter
Emma Site includes information on the four film adaptations of Emma, plus information on connections between the novel and its adaptations. Also has links on Jane Austen (Republic of Pemberley)
Hamlet (BBC Education Page on the filming of Kenneth Branagh's movie. Includes teaching ideas and educational resources)
Love's Labour's Lost Information on the film and cast, along with images and a trailer, commentary by Branagh, and links to educational resources for the film and play (Intermedia)
Mansfield Park This site includes a preview, quotes from Austen, and a set of Jane Austen links (Miramax)
A Midsummer Night's Dream Movie site includes educational resources on the play (Fox)
Restoration (images and clips from the film, accompanied by historical and literary information and texts relevant to the time period) (Miramax)
Richard III: On Stage and Off Information on various film versions of Shakespeare's play, from Looking for Richard to more traditional adaptations (Richard III Society)
Romeo & Juliet (1968 Version) Lots of information on the film, its actors and its production. Plus, reviews, articles, links and commentary (Geocities)
Sense and Sensibility Site includes information on the film version (its cast, filming, location, plot and awards), reviews, a series of essays on adaptating Austen, and general Austen links (Hampshire County Council)
Twelfth Night (film) (includes information on the film, plus a study guide and retail opportunities) (Fine Line Features)
William Shakespeare's Romeo and Juliet (site for the 1996 film with Leonard DiCaprio and Clair Danes; includes information on the author, the play, and the cast, plus images from the film and music files for the entire soundtrack) (Fox, 1996)
